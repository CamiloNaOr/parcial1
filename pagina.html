Tecnology Why do I like artificial intelligence? Published on Artificial intelligence is the technology that seeks to make computers reason like humans. This allows machines to interact with people through natural language, also allows machines to learn from their results and adapt to new environments, and finally solve problems quickly and efficiently, some examples of artificial intelligence are personal assistants such as Siri and Cortana.
There are many types of artificial intelligence one of them is weak artificial intelligence which only focuses on a problem and does not come out of those parameters, by this I mean that it is a type of artificial intelligence that focuses on a very specific problem, an example of this type of artificial intelligence are robots that solve Rubik's cubes, these are capable of solving the cube in any pattern that is found but it is very specific since a robot programmed for a 3x3 cube could not solve a 4x4 or another different type of cube.
There are also machines that are capable of learning these are those that implement machine learning technologies or Deep learning. Starting with machine learning which is the most general set that encompasses the learning of computers, refers to computers that can learn without being explicitly programmed for that, this works since they receive a set of data and through algorithms can recognize patterns between the data and make predictions, thanks to this it can be improved and does not need a person to program explicit instructions on what to do. Some examples of this type of artificial intelligence are the Google search engine or the self-correcting cell phone.
Finally, there is Deep learning being an improved version of Deep Learning. To begin with, just as machine learning receives several data at the beginning, but it is able to adapt to the entry of new data, this is because they imitate the connectivity of the brain. That's called an artificial neural network, which is a set of several algorithms that connect to each other to better verify data and deliver more accurate predictions.
In conclusion I like artificial intelligence since it is a technology that is present everywhere today but still has a high capacity to improve.
